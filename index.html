02 Ecdysis Collective

Co-Founder

Redefining streetwear through bold design and conscious creation.

Ecdysis Collective is a clothing brand born from the idea of transformation — the word "ecdysis" refers to the shedding of an outer layer, a process of becoming something new. We create apparel that speaks to those who refuse to stay the same.

Every piece is designed with intention — merging street culture aesthetics with premium quality and a commitment to standing apart from the noise. Ecdysis isn't just clothing; it's an identity.

Focus Streetwear & Apparel
Philosophy Transformation Through Style
Audience Culture-Forward Creatives

03 Soil Revival CO

Founder

Bringing life back to the ground beneath our feet.

Soil Revival CO is a soil rejuvenation business dedicated to restoring degraded land to its full potential. We work with homeowners, farms, and commercial properties to analyze soil health and implement regenerative solutions that create lasting change.

Using a combination of composting, biochar application, cover cropping guidance, and microbial inoculation, we rebuild the living ecosystem within your soil — improving water retention, nutrient density, and long-term productivity.

Focus Soil Health & Regeneration
Methods Composting, Biochar, Microbial Solutions
Clients Farms, Homeowners, Commercial Properties

04 CARVR Solutions
